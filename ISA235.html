About Me

I'm Tyler Hoffman, a driven and forward-thinking individual currently pursuing my education at Miami University while proudly hailing from the vibrant city of Cleveland, Ohio. My passion for problem-solving and a relentless drive for excellence led me to embark on a journey into the world of consulting.
